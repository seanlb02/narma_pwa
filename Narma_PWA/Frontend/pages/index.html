Having trouble ?

Your pocket-sized support network

Joggr helps people battling anxiety and depression to remain engaged in the real world, no matter how alone they might think they are.

Dreading that next date, work function or friends night out?

We get it, talking isnt easy. That's why your Joggr network is here to help, providing you with ice-breakers, jokes, questions to ask, interesting facts & current affairs. All the conversation starters you could ever need, delivered on the fly via your DMs.

Feeling lonely? Stuck in your own head?

Your Joggr network love randomly sending jokes, memes, trending stories, book and movie recco's and motivational tips they know you'll like. Feel free to share the content with your friends to help you remain engaged in the real world.

No more mindless scrolling, get your tailored content delivered to you in healthy doses.

Need guidance?

Reach out to your Joggr network for some helpful advice & resources that can get you through the hard times. Joggrs can also reccomend you to some mental health professionals in your area, all you have to do is ask.

Start building your Joggr network today for free

1. Download the app and create an account. This is where you can tell the Joggrs a bit about yourself.

2. Start looking for Joggrs to add to your network. Search their profiles and see if you like what they are about.

3. Recieve and send messages to your network. Limit how many messages come through via your preferences.

A new kind of social safety-network

Nothing can replace real people when it comes to mental health support. But sometimes getting daily guidance is challenging when life gets in the way. Joggr offers people a virtual network of allies to help get you through the day-to-day. The Joggr network is developed with science in mind, and designed by professionals with health content contributions coming from certified psychologists.

As always, remain in contact with the people around you for extra help, or Lifeline (13 11 14) in emergencies.
